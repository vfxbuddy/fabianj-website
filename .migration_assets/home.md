## Featured Work (Compositing Supervisor)

### Supergirl — TV, Seasons 5 & 6
As Compositing Supervisor for CW's Supergirl during Seasons 5 and 6, I had the privilege of leading a highly skilled team in finalizing awe-inspiring visual effects for this iconic superhero series under the guidance of our studio leader and VFX Supervisor, Armen Kevorkian.

The ambitious scope of the show demanded a tremendous amount of VFX work, and we faced the added challenge of working within restrictive time schedules typical of episodic television.

Despite these constraints, our team's creative problem-solving abilities enabled us to deliver top-notch results, seamlessly blending live-action footage with CG. The end product was a testament to our commitment to visual excellence, as we crafted scenes that excited DC fans across the globe.

Throughout this exhilarating journey, I honed my skills in strategic project management, coordinating intricate VFX sequences, and fostering a collaborative work environment that inspired innovation and artistic expression.

### Batwoman — TV, Seasons 2 & 3
Working on the renowned CW TV show Batwoman during Seasons 2 and 3 was a remarkable experience that allowed me to contribute my expertise as a Compositing Supervisor. As part of an exceptional team, we collaborated tirelessly to deliver cutting-edge visual effects that complemented the show's compelling narrative.

With a dynamic storyline and a demand for high-quality VFX, the challenges were both invigorating and rewarding. Despite the tight production schedules, our collective dedication and meticulous approach ensured the seamless integration of live-action and CG-elements, elevating the show's visual appeal to new heights.

### Black Lightning — TV, Season 2
Working on the prestigious CW TV show Black Lightning during Season 2 was a gratifying journey. Being part of the team behind this groundbreaking series provided a unique opportunity to contribute to its powerful storytelling and thought-provoking themes.

Navigating the challenges of a fast-paced production, we harnessed our creativity and technical expertise to produce exceptional visual effects that seamlessly enhanced the show's action-packed sequences and dramatic moments.
